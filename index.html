Donut Trivia

When did donuts first appear in the United States?

The history of the donut itself is generally traced to Dutch immigrants in 17th and 18th-century New York, then New Netherland, who prepared fried dough balls called olie koeken or olykoeks, which means “oil cakes.” (Source: Brittanica.com)
